Welcome to my top movie list!

My top 3 movies of all time.


My Neighbor Totoro (1988)

This Studio Ghibli classic, directed by Hayao Miyazaki, follows two young sisters, Satsuki and Mei, who move to the countryside to be closer to their hospitalized mother. They discover magical woodland creatures, including the gentle giant Totoro, who becomes their friend and protector. The story is a heartwarming exploration of childhood wonder, family bonds, and the beauty of nature.


Princess Mononoke (1997)

Another masterpiece by Hayao Miyazaki, Princess Mononoke is an epic tale set in a mythical version of feudal Japan. It follows Ashitaka, a prince cursed by a demonic boar, as he journeys to find a cure. He becomes embroiled in the conflict between the human settlers of Iron Town, led by Lady Eboshi, and the forest spirits and animals, led by San (Princess Mononoke). The film explores themes of environmentalism, industrialization, and the struggle for coexistence.
